Zine 1

This zine playfully compares the perfect day we plan in our heads with the beautifully chaotic reality that actually unfolds. On one side, neat to-do lists promise productivity, balance, and calm. On the other, real life barges in with distractions, detours, missed deadlines, unexpected naps, snack breaks, and spontaneous adventures. Each spread contrasts intention versus outcome in a fun, relatable way, reminding us that it's okay when things don't go exactly as planned. The zine captures this everyday honesty through simple illustrations, warm colours, and a little humour.

Zine 2

"Aam Zindagi" is a playful zine celebrating everyday moments through the lens of the Indian mango. Blending humour, nostalgia, and simple illustrations, the zine explores small stories and rituals connected to mango season. The project focuses on expressive visuals, minimal layouts, and a warm, light-hearted tone that captures the joy of ordinary life in a fun, relatable way.
