Recipe Book

Meals in Minutes is a quick, student-friendly recipe book created to make cooking simple, affordable, and fun for college life. It features easy step-by-step illustrations, minimal ingredients, and time-saving tips designed for busy students living on their own. The project focuses on clean layouts, playful visuals, and accessible instructions that turn everyday meals into something easy and enjoyable to follow.
