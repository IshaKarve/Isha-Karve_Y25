Coloring Book

This project is a festive, child-friendly colouring book created to bring warm holiday moments to life through simple, playful illustrations. Each page features cosy Christmas themes, decorated trees, gifts, reindeer, and gentle winter settings with large, easy-to-colour shapes suitable for young children.

Children’s Book

Buzzy the Bee: The Mystery of the Missing Pollen is a children’s storybook about a curious little bee who sets out to find why the pollen in her garden is disappearing. Through simple illustrations and friendly characters, the story teaches problem-solving and the importance of nature. The project explores character design, visual storytelling and clear, engaging layouts for young readers.
